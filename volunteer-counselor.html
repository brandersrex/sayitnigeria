Become a Counselor.

Volunteer to Become a Counselor At SayiT, we believe in the power of shared experiences and the importance of mental well-being. Our platform is designed to empower individuals to express their thoughts, feelings, and experiences in a safe, supportive environment, where their voices are not only heard but understood.

As a counselor on SayiT, you will play a pivotal role in fostering this environment.
We are looking for passionate, empathetic individuals who are dedicated to making a positive impact on others' lives by providing guidance, support, and a listening ear.

Whether you have years of professional experience or are just starting your journey in mental health advocacy, your contribution is invaluable to us. By volunteering as a counselor, you will help us continue our mission to promote mental well-being and provide crucial support to individuals who need it most. You'll also have the opportunity to grow personally and professionally, gaining experience in counseling while contributing to a cause that truly matters. We are calling on individuals with a background in psychology, counseling, therapy, or any relevant field to join our community of volunteers. Your time and expertise can make a significant difference in someone's life.
