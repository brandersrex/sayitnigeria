Our Services

At SayiT, we offer a range of services designed to support mental well-being, personal growth, and emotional resilience. Whether you're seeking guidance, emotional support, or simply a listening ear, our services are tailored to meet your unique needs. Here’s how we can help:



1. Anonymous Sharing : Sometimes, opening up can be difficult. Our platform allows you to share your thoughts, struggles, and feelings anonymously, giving you the freedom to express yourself without fear of judgment. Whether it’s mental health challenges, emotional pain, or personal concerns, you can speak your truth in a safe, non-judgmental environment.



2. One-on-One Counseling :
We connect you with experienced and empathetic counselors who specialize in various areas, including mental health, emotional healing, addiction recovery, self-esteem, and purpose discovery. Our one-on-one sessions are confidential and personalized to ensure you receive the care and guidance that best suits your needs.


3. Group Counseling Sessions :
Our group counseling sessions provide a supportive space for individuals to connect with others facing similar challenges. Led by professional counselors, these sessions encourage collective healing, shared experiences, and community support. Group topics range from mental health and relationships to personal growth and self-awareness.


4. Workshops and Webinars :
We offer workshops and webinars focused on mental well-being, personal development, and life skills. Our experts cover topics such as stress management, building resilience, emotional intelligence, and finding purpose. These sessions are designed to equip you with practical tools for navigating life’s challenges.


5. Mental Health Resources :
Our platform provides access to a wealth of mental health resources, including articles, self-help tools, and coping strategies for dealing with a variety of issues such as anxiety, depression, stress, and trauma. These resources are curated to support your journey towards mental wellness.


6. Volunteer to Become a Counselor :
If you’re passionate about mental health and helping others, our platform offers an opportunity to become a volunteer counselor. Join our network of dedicated professionals and make a positive impact in the lives of others by offering guidance, support, and a listening ear.


7. Crisis Support :
SayiT is not just a platform; it’s a community. Our peer support services connect you with others who understand your struggles and can provide encouragement and camaraderie. Together, we build a space where everyone feels heard, valued, and supported.


8. Community and Peer Support :
For individuals in urgent need of assistance, our crisis support service offers immediate access to qualified counselors who can provide guidance during critical moments. This service ensures that help is available when it’s needed most.



At SayiT, we are committed to fostering an environment where everyone can find their voice, receive the help they need, and take steps towards a healthier, happier future. Whether you're in Nigeria or anywhere in the world, we are here to support you on your journey.

Let us walk with you—together, we can make a difference.
